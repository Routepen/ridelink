What is the initial deposit?

We want to help you, as a driver, with last minute bailing and payments, so you won't have to worry about riders not paying or ditching you for another driver. But keep in mind, to make all this possible for you, riders have to pay an additional $2 or $3. We believe it's a small price to pay for the security and legitimacy we provide. If you're not comfortable with this extra surcharge, go ahead and toggle initial deposit off! Keep in mind! You don't lose any money during this process
